Accommodation

Delegates travelling from outside Aberdeen have the option of free accommodation on the night of the 27th near RGU's Garthdee campus.

Check-in is from 4pm onwards, with bus transport organised between the conference venue and the accommodation. Breakfast is not included, but can be added on at the hotel.

If you need to make your own way to the accommodation at any point, the address is *Aberdeen address*. Check-out time is 11am, and delegates can store their luggage safely within rooms at RGU after this point. More information on accomodation for the conference will be added shortly.
