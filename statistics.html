5 Statistical Mistakes You are Likely to Make

Presenter: TBA
Organisers: Lovisa Sundin ;

Lecture slides will become available here

Abstract

This workshop will give students an insight into how easy it is for statistical misconceptions to represent data incorrectly. Going beyond “correlation does not equal causation”, the workshop will cover common mistakes like relative vs. absolute risk, excessive precision, and effect size neglect. The students will also participate in an exercise of re-writing abstracts in a sensationalised form. This will highlight just how easy it is to exaggerate using statistics, and how it can be avoided.
